Security Hall of Fame

At Faylotto, we are committed to maintaining a secure and trustworthy platform. We value the efforts of the global security community in helping us identify and resolve potential vulnerabilities.

As of now, we have not received any valid vulnerability reports. This page will be updated to recognize individuals who responsibly disclose any issues that help us improve our platform.

If you believe you’ve found a vulnerability or security issue, please follow the disclosure instructions in our security.txt file.

We appreciate your cooperation in protecting our users and the Web3 community.
